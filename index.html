JackFish Software

JackFish is a Certified Microsoft® Mediaroom Application Development partner. JackFish works in partnership with carriers to empower them by addressing challenges they may face as they reinvent themselves as video providers. JackFish software and services simplify B/OSS integration and lower the operational burden to provision and support Microsoft® Mediaroom JackFish helps carriers customize and manage their IPTV offerings with unique operational and consumer applications:

JackFish PlantTM

JackFish PlantTM is a suite of Mediaroom Integration and Management Applications that enables service providers to rapidly integrate the necessary systems and applications to offer high-quality IPTV services, protect the Quality of Service (QoS) and manage the overall network Mediaroom implementation from one common interface utilizing state of the art software engines.


Plant Applications reside on a minimum of two servers dependent on the level of desired redundancy.
